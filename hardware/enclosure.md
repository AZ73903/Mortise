# Mortise — Enclosure

*Dimensions and assembly details to be documented during build.*

## Three-Piece System

**Mortise (robot body)**
- Walnut and maple, handcrafted
- Rubio Monocoat finish
- Brass hardware throughout
- Houses: Jetson Orin, BT30D amp PCB, PCM5102 DAC, CPX, speaker eyes, front panel, head assembly

**Base Platform / Legs**
- Houses: PSUs (Jetson wall wart, 24A PSU, 24V→5V buck), IEC inlet, wagos
- Single IEC inlet, one cable to wall

**Sub Orb**
- Lathe-turned walnut sphere
- Houses: Dayton Audio RS125P-8 5" woofer, rear facing
- Speaker wire connects to base / BT30D amp
